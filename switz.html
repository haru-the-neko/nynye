Dream Places

Switzerland

Why This Place is Special to Me

I want to visit Switzerland for its breathtaking alpine landscapes, picturesque lakes, and charming villages. The country offers a perfect escape with its snowy mountains, scenic train rides, and rich cultural heritage. And because it feels like a real-life version of the Studio Ghibli films I watched growing up, visiting Switzerland has always been a dream of mine.

How to Get There

To get to Switzerland from Manila, take a connecting flight with airlines like Emirates, Qatar Airways, or Singapore Airlines, usually with layovers in cities like Dubai or Doha. The flight takes around 16 to 20 hours, arriving at Zurich or Geneva Airport. From there, use the efficient Swiss train system or taxis for transport. Ensure you have a Schengen visa, use Swiss Francs (CHF), and consider a Swiss Travel Pass for convenience. Pack for the season, and while German, French, and Italian are spoken, English is commonly understood.

Other Places to Visit Nearby

  • Zermatt and the Matterhorn - A picturesque mountain village offering stunning views of the iconic Matterhorn. Ideal for skiing, snowboarding, and scenic hikes.
  • Lucerne - Known for its charming old town, the Chapel Bridge, and the serene Lake Lucerne. Take a boat cruise or explore nearby Mount Pilatus.
  • Interlaken - A paradise for adventure seekers, nestled between Lake Thun and Lake Brienz. Enjoy paragliding, hiking, or take a train ride to Jungfraujoch, the "Top of Europe."
